🧠 The Basics

What is AI?

Understanding the fundamentals in plain English

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is like a computer program that can learn and make decisions, kind of like how humans do. But instead of a brain, it uses math and huge amounts of examples to figure things out.

Think of it like teaching a friend to recognize cats by showing them thousands of cat photos. Eventually, they spot the patterns — pointy ears, whiskers, tail — and can identify a cat in a photo they've never seen before. That's basically how AI works: you feed it data, it finds patterns, and then it can make predictions about new stuff.

YouTube Recommendations

YouTube watches what videos you like, how long you watch them, and what you skip. It learns your taste and suggests similar videos you might enjoy.

Face Unlock

Your phone creates a mathematical map of your facial features. When you look at the camera, it compares this map to unlock only for you.

Computer Vision

Duolingo Lessons

Duolingo tracks which words you struggle with and which you know well. It adapts future lessons to focus on what you need to practice most.
